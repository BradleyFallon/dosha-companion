# Product Overview

> Understand your nature. Track your balance. Know what supports you today.

## Product definition

Ayurvedic Wellness Companion is a personalized, mobile-first wellness web app. It helps users learn about their baseline constitution, reflect on their current balance, and discover expert-reviewed practices that may support them today.

## Experience model

- No human coach is included in the product experience.
- Expert-authored content provides the foundational Ayurvedic knowledge.
- An LLM may personalize explanations and retrieval, within approved rules and safety boundaries.
- A continuous assessment refines the profile and checks changeable conditions over time.
- Daily guidance is selected from expert-approved content by deterministic rules. An LLM may personalize its explanation and presentation but may not invent the underlying health guidance.
- The primary experience is a mobile-first responsive web app.

## Intended user value

Users should be able to understand the product's interpretation of their tendencies, see how confident and current that interpretation is, and choose small, realistic wellness actions without being led to believe they have received a diagnosis.
