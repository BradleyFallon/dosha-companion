# Safety Boundaries

## Product boundary

This product provides general wellness education and self-reflection. It is not medical care and its assessment does not identify a medical condition.

## The product must not

- Diagnose or imply a diagnosis
- Treat, cure, prevent, or manage a disease
- Provide emergency guidance beyond directing the user to appropriate emergency services
- Recommend starting, stopping, or changing medication
- Provide personalized herbal or supplement prescriptions in the MVP
- Claim that quiz results identify a disease, disorder, deficiency, or clinical imbalance
- Discourage care from a qualified clinician
- Present generated text as expert-reviewed when it is not

## Escalation

When a user describes urgent or potentially dangerous symptoms, the experience should stop wellness personalization, clearly state its limits, and direct the user to local emergency services or an appropriate qualified professional. Do not attempt to assess severity.
