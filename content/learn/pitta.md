Pitta is commonly described through qualities such as warmth, sharpness, intensity, and transformation. Traditional descriptions may connect these qualities with appetite, focus, and other patterns.

These associations are broad educational ideas, not medical findings. Context matters, and a recent experience should not be used alone to assign a lasting label.
