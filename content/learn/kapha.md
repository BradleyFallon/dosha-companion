Kapha is commonly described through qualities such as steadiness, heaviness, coolness, smoothness, and stability. Traditional descriptions may connect these qualities with endurance, structure, and other patterns.

These associations are broad educational ideas, not medical findings. Different qualities can appear together, and no single observation establishes a dosha profile.
