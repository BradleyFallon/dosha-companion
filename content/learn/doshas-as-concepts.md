Ayurvedic traditions use Vata, Pitta, and Kapha as organizing concepts. They are often used to describe clusters of qualities, tendencies, and patterns rather than isolated facts.

## Educational, not diagnostic

In this app, dosha language is educational. No label is inferred from your answers. A single answer should never be treated as proof of a constitution or imbalance.

## Patterns need context

Age, climate, culture, routine, illness, medication, and recent life changes can all affect self-report. That is why the assessment keeps usual nature separate from recent experience and preserves an honest “not sure” option.
